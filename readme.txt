Convert your blog into a podcast.

== Description ==

Reach audiences who prefer to listen! Automatically convert your blog into a beautifully sounding audio and add players directly to your website. To use this pulgin you need to have an active account on the HearMe.ai platform.

This plugin provides easy integration with HearMe.ai platform to generate audio for your articles or posts. In order to use this service you need to have an account at [HearMe.ai APP](https://app.hearme.ai). If you don't already have it, just follow the link and register. 

Your privacy is important to us. See full [Privacy Policy](https://hearme.ai/privacy-policy/)

== Installation ==
If you have any questions or would like to raise an issue please contact hello@hearme.com.

= Minimum Requirements =

Register your account on [HearMe.ai APP](https://app.hearme.ai) and add your blog's link.
After installing the plugin you will be automatically moved to settings (Settings -> HearMe Settings).
You have to insert API Key, save and click "Generae draft for all posts". After that you can use HearMe and convert your blog.
